Knowledge Base

Your questions, answered

What does BrandBox actually include? expand_more
AI Store Builder for niche-based Shopify setup, Product Vault with AI-chosen products you can import and push, plus coach chat (AI replies when a coach isn’t available).
Do I need a Shopify store first? expand_more
Yes. Create a Shopify store (or use one you already have), then connect it to BrandBox so you can build, import products, and push updates.
Is BrandBox good if I’m new to e-commerce? expand_more
Yes. The builder walks you through niche setup, the vault helps you choose products, and coach chat is there when you get stuck.
Does BrandBox create ads for me? expand_more
No. BrandBox focuses on store building, product selection, Shopify sync, and coaching — not ad generation.
Do you handle shipping and fulfillment? expand_more
No. BrandBox helps you set up and stock your Shopify store. Shipping and fulfillment stay with you and your Shopify / supplier setup.
What’s the difference between a coach and the AI chat? expand_more
Coaches are people who help when available. If no coach is free, BrandBox AI answers so you still get guidance on setup, niches, and products.
What is the Summer Promo? expand_more
Eligible customers can get 65% off Plus Annual for the first year. Check checkout for the current price and what’s included on your plan.
Can I change products after the store is built? expand_more
Yes. Keep browsing the Product Vault, import more items, and push updates to Shopify whenever you want to refresh your catalog.
